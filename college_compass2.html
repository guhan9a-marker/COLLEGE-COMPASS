About the College

St. Joseph’s College of Engineering, with 29 years of academic distinction, offers 11 UG and 7 PG programs taking in more than 1,570 students per year. The college is well-known for its outstanding ranking records, producing over 1,541 university ranks and multiple gold and silver medals. It has held first place for student ranks among over 500 institutions under Anna University three times.

  • Ranked in the Top 10 for Anna University ranks, first place achieved in three different years.
  • Nationally ranked under NIRF since 2017 and ARIIA ‘Elite Band’ for innovation in 2021.
  • Accredited A+ by NAAC (3.46/4.0 rating).
  • ‘Autonomous Status’ under Anna University since 2020 for most UG programs.
  • Many departments recognized as Anna University Research Centres, with ongoing Ph.D. and MS scholars.
  • Strong focus on research, multiple funded research projects, and a high h-index in national and international journals.
  • Multiple national awards (AICTE, ARIIA, MHRD) for innovation, women empowerment, and employability excellence.

Vision

To become a world-class educational center for engineering, technology, computer applications, and management, fostering high moral and professional standards to benefit the wider community.

Mission

  • Achieve academic excellence across all disciplines.
  • Inculcate strong moral and professional values.
  • Develop students’ overall personality.
  • Strengthen industry-institute collaboration and ensure high campus placement rates.

Core Values

  • Reverence: Inclusive and caring environment valuing diversity of the staff and student community.
  • Excellence: Commitment to academic quality and innovative learning.
  • Liability: Transparent administration, accountability, and ongoing evaluation of institutional practices.
  • Discipline: Promotion of human values, gender equity, cultural traditions, and responsible citizenship.
